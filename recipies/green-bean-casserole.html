Grandmas Green Bean Casserole

Description

Green bean casserole is a delicious easy side dish with green beans in a delisious creamy cheesy mushroom sauce topped with crispy fried onions and melted cheese that can be thrown together in just a few quick

Ingredients

  • 1 10.5oz can of cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 14.5oz cans of green beans
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 6oz tin of fried onions

Directions

  1. preheat oven to 350F (175C)
  2. mix together the drained green beans and mushroom soup im a microwave safe bowl
  3. microwave on high until warm 2-3 minuites
  4. stir in half cup cheddar cheese and microwave again for 1-2 minuites to help incorperate the cheese into the sauce
