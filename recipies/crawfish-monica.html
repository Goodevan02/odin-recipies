Crawfish Monica

Description

a tasty crawfish pasta originating in new orleans that was popularized by the jazz and heritage festival. The dish is a tasty yet simple pasta dish with a rich and creamy sauce infused with seasoned crawfish tails stirred into rotini pasta

This dish is also extremely similar to crawfish fettucine which is another crawfish pasta dish popular in southern louisiana

Ingredients

  • peeled cooked crawfish tails
  • Rotini Pasta
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • creole/cajun seasoning(I reccomend Tony Chachere's or Emeril's)
  • White wine
  • Heavy cream
  • Shallots
  • Lemon juice
  • Green onions
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Peel crawfish leaving just the tails
  2. Cook the rotini according to the package directions
  3. In a large pot or dutch oven melt butter over medium high heat
  4. Add chopped shallots and cook till they are translucent
  5. Add minced garlic and cook for 2 mins until fragrant
  6. Add seasoning and stir well until combined
  7. Stir in white wine
  8. Slowly add in heavy cream
  9. Mix in lemon juice
  10. Stir in crawfish tails
  11. Stir in chopped parsley and green onions
  12. Mix in parmesan cheese and cook for a further 3 minutes until melted
  13. Stir in cooked pasta mix well until combined and serve immediately
