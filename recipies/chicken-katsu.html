chicken katsu

A Tasty fried breadcrumb covered chicken cutlet that goes well with seaweed salad and rice

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • fry oil

Steps

  1. slice the chicken breast in half along the width of the breast
  2. place in between two sheets of plastic wrap
  3. pound each cutlet to 1/2 an inch thick with a mallet
  4. salt both sides of each cutlet and wait 1-2 hours
  5. set up a standard egg wash station with separate flour, egg, and panko dishes
  6. coat each cutlet in flour then egg then panko breadcrumbs
  7. heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  8. place breaded chicken cutlets in hot oil and fry until golden brown (approx 3-4 mins per side)
  9. let drain or cool over a wire rack or paper towel lined plate/tray
